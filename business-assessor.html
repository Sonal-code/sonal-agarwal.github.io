Tell me about the business

Paste in anything the agent or seller has sent you — the listing, brochure, job ads, emails, anything. The more detail, the better the assessment.

💰 Financial information

The most important step. Answer what you can — skip anything you don't have yet.

💡 Turnover vs profit? Turnover = total money coming IN. Profit = what's left after paying all bills. Both matter a lot.

👥 Staff & how it runs

Understanding day-to-day operations is crucial before buying.

💡 Key person risk: If the business only works because of one person (often the owner), that's a major red flag. You need it to run without them.
